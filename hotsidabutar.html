Berikut cara membingkai keahlian Anda secara strategis:Communicating with C-suite level partners requires a shift from technical details to business impact. Here’s how to frame your expertise strategically:

1. Gunakan Bahasa "Output & Hasil"1. Use "Output & Outcome" Language

Salah:Wrong: "I installed an IoT monitoring system on the mining truck fleet."

Benar (Strategis):Right (Strategic): "I integrated Real-Time Data Analytics that successfully reduced operational costs by 15% and improved fuel efficiency across the entire fleet."

"Technically, Company A is currently leading in mine automation in Indonesia. If our client wants to win in this region, they need a leader with a proven track record in Digital Transformation, like person X at that company."

3. Ringkas dan Langsung ke Inti (Bottom Line Up Front)3. Be Concise (Bottom Line Up Front)

Mulai dengan kesimpulan, lalu berikan detail jika ditanya. Contohnya:Start with the conclusion, then provide details if asked. For example:

"Based on my technical assessment, this candidate scores high on Innovation (GC Index), but lacks understanding of on-site Safety Protocols. My recommendation: they will need mentorship in the first 3 months."
